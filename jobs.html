Who we are

We are a venture-funded, engineering-focused firm in Palo Alto, California. Our products make ID verification and anti-fraud accessible to every business using modern technologies and APIs. Our founders come from Stanford and Duke, and founded the Stanford Bitcoin Group. We encourage cross-disciplinary participation with every team member touching every part of the business.

Compliance Officer
- Full Time - Palo Alto, CA

Compliance stands at the core of BlockScore's offerings. You will be responsible for the provision of compliance services to the company.

As part of working here, you will interface with our world-class lawyers, our data vendors, local governments, national governments and our customers to answer questions, create contracts and keep up to date with the global compliance landscape. We work with data vendors across the globe, each with their own privacy implications and surrounding laws and it will be your job to stay on top of what's new and make sure that the product and on-boarding requirements align with industry best practices.

Requirements

  • Bachelor Degree in related field
  • 2 - 5 years of compliance experience preferred
  • Knowledge of OFAC, AML and sanctions
  • Strong writing and communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and in a team
