When and how am I billed?

You are billed on the first of the month for the transactions you completed during the prior month. Our default payment method is credit card, but additional options are available on request.

How does the pricing work?

For our pay as you go plan, you pay for each person or company that you verify. If you decide to expand the scope of the verification, each transaction can cost a bit more.

What constitutes "high volume"?

The amount that defines high volume can vary quite heavily based on the product and the geographical distribution of your customers. Don't hesitate to contact us to get an individualized quote.

Why does international data cost more than US data?

Due to the more advanced nature of US public record and credit infrastructure, data prices are generally significantly lower than other countries. Because of these increased international costs, we have to compensate accordingly.
